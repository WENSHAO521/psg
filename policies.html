Publishing Policies

Peer Review Policy

All journals published by Panorama Scholarly Group employ a peer review process appropriate to their academic scope and discipline. Editorial decisions are based solely on scholarly merit, originality, and relevance.

Open Access Policy

Panorama Scholarly Group supports open-access publishing principles and provides free and unrestricted access to published content, subject to the applicable copyright and licensing terms of each journal.

Copyright and Licensing

Copyright arrangements and licensing terms are specified at the journal level. Authors retain copyright to their work unless otherwise stated, and licenses are clearly indicated in published articles.

Archiving Policy

The publisher is committed to the long-term preservation of scholarly content. Digital archiving strategies may include institutional repositories and other recognized preservation services.

Corrections and Retractions

Panorama Scholarly Group follows established academic standards for issuing corrections, retractions, or expressions of concern where necessary to maintain the integrity of the scholarly record.
