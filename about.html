About the Publisher

Panorama Scholarly Group (PSG) is an independent open-access academic publishing initiative dedicated to advancing transparent, ethical, and accessible scholarly communication. The publisher aims to provide a stable and credible platform for the dissemination of peer-reviewed academic research.

Mission

The mission of Panorama Scholarly Group is to support high-quality academic publishing through rigorous editorial standards, transparent peer review processes, and open-access dissemination models that promote the broad accessibility of research outcomes.

Publishing Scope

Panorama Scholarly Group publishes peer-reviewed academic journals across the humanities, social sciences, and interdisciplinary research fields. Each journal operates within its defined academic scope and maintains editorial independence under the guidance of its respective editorial board.

Editorial Independence and Governance

All journals published by Panorama Scholarly Group are managed by independent editorial boards. Editorial decisions are made solely on academic merit and relevance, without influence from the publisher or external interests. The publisher is committed to upholding internationally recognized standards of publication ethics and academic integrity.

Open Access Commitment

Panorama Scholarly Group supports open-access publishing principles and seeks to ensure that scholarly research is freely available to the academic community and the public, in accordance with applicable licensing and copyright policies.
